Pravasi ID Card

The Pravasi Identity Card, issued by Norka Roots, serves as an identification document for Keralites residing or working abroad. Cardholders are entitled to insurance coverage, including Rs 5 lakh in the event of accidental death and a maximum of Rs 2 lakh for partial or permanent disability resulting from an accident.

For NRKs Outside India

Student ID Card

Student ID is an identity card for Keralite students studying abroad, launched by Norka Roots in April 2020. The ID cardholder will be eligible for Personal Accident Insurance coverage of Rs. 5 Lakhs in the case of death and up to a maximum of Rs. 2 Lakhs for permanent or partial disability.

For Students Abroad

NRK ID Card

NRK Insurance Card introduced in June 2012 to serve as an Identity Card for NRKs residing in other states. NRK Insurance Card holders will be eligible for Personal Accident Insurance coverage of Rs. 5 Lakhs and up to a maximum of Rs. 2 Lakhs for permanent or partial disability.

For NRKs Inside India

NPRI

Norka Pravasi Raksha Insurance Policy launched by Norka Roots, for providing Critical illness care to NRKs. A sum of Rs. 1 lakh is provided for critical illness (as per schedule) on the submission of a medical certificate from a registered medical practitioner. In addition to critical illness care, add-on benefit of Accident Insurance coverage of Rs. 3 lakhs for life and up to Rs. 1 lakh for permanent/ partial disability.
